Production Brief

Creator brief, format spec, talent

  • Tone: conversational and lived-in, not performed. The flatter the better.
  • Delivery: first-person; the viewer should feel reflected, not addressed.
  • Camera: eye-level, minimal background, natural light. No ring-light gloss.

  • Never mention subscription pricing or competitor names on camera.
  • POV concept (07): never feels like an ad until the reveal beat.
  • Stop Scrolling (06): produced in-house at 36N. Single shot, phone-held camera, lived-in setting. No b-roll needed.

Channel Priority

Apple Search — the only clean read right now

Why it matters

Apple Search is the only platform with intact iOS attribution this cycle. Meta and TikTok install data is unreliable until Singular is removed from Meta BM. Apple is also the most efficient channel in the account.

  • $13.18 blended install CPA
  • $10.08 CPA on Young Pro — the sharpest segment in the account
  • 15.12% click-to-install rate (17 installs from 172 clicks)
  • 5.7× better than the $75 CPA target
Recommendation for this cycle
  • Scale Apple Search through keyword pruning and expansion — not just budget increase.
  • Weight winning Young Pro keywords (daily learning app, productivity, branded demand).
  • Reduce broad-match exposure on weaker HISI keywords.
  • Use Apple Search to validate creative angles while Meta attribution is broken — downstream conversion is reliable here.
  • Run Concept 10 Execution A (Intelligent Companion) on Apple Display — premium static fits search-intent context cleanly.
